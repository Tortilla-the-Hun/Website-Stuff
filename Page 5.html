This page will talk about my education. I graduated from High School in 2008 in a time some would a call a technology golden-age. Vista was there to tempt people away from trusty Windows XP, MySpace was huge and gave me the inspiration to even start coding with HTML, and the Wii was sold out for almost a year straight. In 2014, I decided to dip my toes in college for the first time, attending Anoka Technical Institute, and was overwhelmed with the amount of homework I received. I ended up dropping out halfway through my first semester.

In 2017, after getting laid off from my powder-coat painting job and sick of the warehouse gigs, I decided to go for my CompTIA A+ Certification, which was a year long ordeal with pre-requisites to make before being admitted. Frustrated with the IT job market, I became a line cook to make ends meet, until 2024 when I started my current job. I went back to school at NHCC in 2025 and learned SQL my first semester. In the future, I will be looking into C/C++, C#, Javascript, Python, OOL, and a few others.
