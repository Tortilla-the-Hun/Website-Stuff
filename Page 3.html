I've spoken about gaming quite a bit, but another passion of mine is music. I was a Internet Radio DJ for about 6 months before continuing my education, and it sparked a creative interest in me. I do not have the fancy turntables and dj controllers yet, but it's on my to-do list. I've been to a decent amount of concerts, both big and small. My cousin introduced me to rap at a young age in the mid 90's, and I only listened to metal and punk in high school, with some Pink Floyd and Led Zeppelin mixed in for good measure. The only kind of music I don't really listen to is country, though I'll make an exception for Johnny Cash.
