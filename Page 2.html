As I'm sure you've noticed in my first page, I am a gamer, first and foremost. My very first game was Castlevania III at the tender age of 2, when my cousin let me play his NES while babysitting me. 2 years later, he gifted me that same NES for Christmas, along with Blades of Steel, the Mario/Duck Hunt combo cart, and Rambo. Some of the most influential games in my life have been Halo, which introduced me to LAN parties and competitive gaming; Zelda, which inspired to create art; and Destiny, which introduced me to content creation.
